ABOUT S.A.T.I

Innovating for a Digital Future

Sarawak Advanced Technology Institute (S.A.T.I) is a leading educational institution specializing in Artificial Intelligence, Blockchain, and Robotics. Our mission is to prepare students for the challenges of the digital economy through a comprehensive curriculum, state-of-the-art facilities, and real-world project experiences. We are committed to producing graduates who are not only tech-savvy but also innovative thinkers ready to contribute to the global digital transformation.

OUR PROGRAMS

Dedicated to excellence in teaching, learning, and research

At S.A.T.I, we offer a range of programs designed to equip students with the skills and knowledge needed to excel in the fields of Artificial Intelligence, Blockchain, and Robotics. Our curriculum is developed in collaboration with industry experts to ensure relevance and applicability in the real world. Students can engage in hands-on learning experiences through our state-of-the-art labs, workshops, and collaborative research projects.

  • Artificial Intelligence: Dive into the world of AI with courses covering machine learning, neural networks, and cognitive computing.
  • Blockchain: Explore the fundamentals of blockchain technology, cryptocurrency, and distributed ledger systems.
  • Robotics: Gain practical experience in robotics design, programming, and systems integration.
