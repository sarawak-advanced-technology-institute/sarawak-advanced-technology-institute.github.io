COLLABORATIVE INNOVATION



Under the auspices of the Sarawak Digital Economy Blueprint, SATI has carved out its role as a pivotal education and human capital development enabler. Recognizing that digital skills are the cornerstone of the Post COVID-19 Development Strategy, SATI is dedicated to accelerating the acquisition of digital talents across the industry spectrum. To amplify its impact, strategic initiatives such as the Human Capital Development Council and the Sarawak Workforce Information System (SWIS) are being strengthened, alongside the development of International Schools emphasizing Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics (STEM) education.

The institute is not just a vision but a confluence of expertise and global experience. With team members who have honed their skills at organizations like Google AI/Robotics and Boston Dynamics, SATI aims to make Sarawak a hub for digital education, not just in Malaysia but across the region. It offers a trinity of faculties in Blockchain, AI, and Robotics, integrated into a Sarawak Campus that is slated to provide a melting pot of innovation and learning.

SATI’s business model in Sarawak is a collaborative effort in partnership with educational powerhouses such as Unimas and MIT. The goal is to weave advanced learning in AI, Blockchain, and Robotics into the educational fabric of Sarawak, backed by industry and academic giants like MIT and Boston Dynamics. SATI’s educational model bridges the gap between local and international universities, providing certificate to bachelor programs aimed at cultivating versatile and industry-ready professionals.

The student journey at SATI is meticulously crafted to transition students from enrolment to global job markets. This includes rigorous examination processes, enriching intern programs supported by the Sarawak government, and a blend of local campus and online learning to cater to diverse needs. Upon graduation, students are ushered into a world of opportunities, supported by the SWIS and partnerships with industry players and research institutes.

The learning modules at SATI are diverse and comprehensive, covering areas like Machine Learning, Neural Networks, Cryptocurrencies, Distributed Ledger Technology, Robotics Programming, and Navigation, with an emphasis on real-world applications through capstone projects like AnetaBTC, TeddySwap, CherryLend, and TosiDrop.

The benefits of joining SATI are multifold. From paid internships and access to cutting-edge technology to mentorship programs and career placement services, the institute is dedicated to providing a holistic and supportive educational environment. Students can also engage in collaborative research projects, participate in networking events, and gain recognition through awards and certificates.

As SATI gears up for its rollout, the timeline is set with strategic milestones including company setup, memoranda of agreement with universities, and the establishment of a robust business operation. This paves the way for a sustainable revenue stream encompassing tuition fees, research grants, endowments, and government funding, further bolstering SATI’s position as an educational paragon.

SARAWAK EDUCATION ENABLERS



Digital skills are key to digital transformation and a significant enabler of Sarawak’s Post COVID-19 Development Strategy 2030 and Digital Economy 2030 agenda. Accelerating the necessary digital talent and skills have become a key part of Sarawak’s digital transformation strategies. The demand for digitally skilled workers will continue to grow as the digitalisation accelerates across all industry sectors.

Tailoring education to support human capital development is important to increase work efficiency and drive economies to move up the value chain. The key initiatives include the formation of Human Capital Development Council and strengthening Sarawak Workforce Information System (SWIS), establishment of Science Centre to increase interest in Science, Technology, Engineering and Mathematics (STEM) and the establishment of five (5) International Schools in Sarawak.
